The technology already exists in homes like yours.
Most people just haven't activated it.

Home batteries, smart thermostats, virtual power plants, and demand response programs could save U.S. consumers over $100 billion in the next decade. The U.S. grid runs at 53% capacity. You're paying for the other 47% whether you use it or not.

The gap isn't access. It isn't technology. It's adoption — and adoption fails at a specific moment, for a specific reason, that's different for every person.

This tool has one job: identify the single action that moves you from where you are to the next stage. Not a product comparison. Not a sales funnel. A diagnostic. No contact info required at any point.
